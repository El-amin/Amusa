About Me

I am an Assistant Lecturer at the Federal University, Dutse, Nigeria. My research spans Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on machine learning, medical imaging, and domain adaptation techniques for improving model fairness and generalization across diverse populations. I am the founder of the CRAI AI Research Lab, where I lead efforts to build inclusive AI capacity in underrepresented communities. Aminu is passionate about applying deep learning to solve real-world problems and has contributed to projects in healthcare, education, and agriculture.
