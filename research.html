Core Research Focus

Medical Imaging Domain Adaptation

Domain Shift in Chest X-ray Imaging

Addresses the challenges of domain shift and cross-population discrepancies in medical image classification. Using deep supervised domain adaptation and adversarial training (such as Supervised Adversarial Domain Adaptation), this project aims to mitigate the performance drop of classification models when applied to datasets from new clinical settings or underrepresented populations.

Multi-task Learning Medical AI Fairness

Multi-task Learning for Medical AI

Investigates multi-task deep learning architectures (like FairCXRnet) to solve multiple clinical tasks simultaneously (e.g., detecting multiple thoracic diseases from chest X-rays). This approach helps models leverage shared feature representations, improving generalization and fairness, especially in resource-constrained environments.

EdTech Community Engagement

AI Literacy Training for 2,700 Teachers in Jigawa

A capacity-building community initiative to train 2,700 public school teachers in Jigawa State, Nigeria on AI literacy, digital pedagogy, and basic computing tools. Led under the CRAI AI Research Lab, this project aims to bridge the digital divide and empower educators to leverage modern technology in their classrooms.

Open Source & Engineering Projects

Vision-Language Model MedGemma

NaijaCXR-VLM

A specialized vision-language model (VLM) based on MedGemma for interpreting chest X-ray images. Fine-tuned using Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) and adversarial domain adaptation on both the NaijaCXR and MIMIC-CXR datasets to generate high-quality diagnostic reports.

NLP BERT

HauwBert

A fine-tuned multilingual BERT (mBERT) transformer model optimized for aspect-based sentiment analysis of Hausa-language text (e.g., movie reviews). This project demonstrates the adaptation of large language models to low-resource languages.

Knowledge Distillation Edge AI

Lightweight Pothole Detection

Part of an MS thesis, this project utilizes knowledge distillation techniques to train a lightweight convolutional neural network (CNN) for detecting potholes from highway imagery, enabling real-time deployment on resource-constrained embedded systems and IoT devices.

Smart Agriculture TensorFlow

Intelligent Plant Disease Detection

An implementation of a deep learning system for smart hydroponic farming. It utilizes convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to analyze leaf images, detecting crop diseases early to improve agricultural yields in automated farming setups.
